Understanding Phishing Attacks and Maintaining Social Hygiene

Phishing attacks are malicious attempts to deceive individuals into revealing sensitive information.

Learn how to recognize and prevent such attacks :

  • Check the Sender
  • Be Cautious of Links
  • Avoid Sharing Personal Information
  • Double-Check Requests
  • Use Strong Passwords
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  • Keep Software Updated
  • Educate Yourself

For maintaining social hygiene in the digital age, consider the following :

  • Privacy Settings
  • Think Before You Share
  • Limit Public Information
  • Regularly Review Accounts
  • Be Skeptical
  • Secure Wi-Fi Connections
  • Regularly Monitor Accounts
